Quick deck loading

  • MTGStocks: ?mtgstocksdeckid=(id)
  • MTGGoldfish: ?mtggoldfishdeckid=(id)

Card shortcuts

  • b: put card on the bottom of library
  • c: add counter to card
  • d: draw a card
  • e: put card in exile
  • f: flip card
  • g: put card in graveyard
  • l: put card on the top of library
  • m: mark/highlight a card
  • t: tap card
